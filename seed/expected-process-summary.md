Atlas mapped two parallel admissions workflows that run side by side. In the Individual Recruiting Funnel, James Carter's recruiting team sources and screens prospective families, and every applicant is then routed through a manual Eligibility Verification step before moving on to enrollment. In the Scholarship and Financial Aid Program, Priya Nair's team reviews scholarship applications, which are also routed through that same Eligibility Verification step before award determination and payment. Maria Lopez, the Enrollment Coordinator, owns that verification step and runs it on a single legacy spreadsheet she maintains by hand. Both flows therefore converge on one person and one system at the eligibility checkpoint — the point where the two otherwise-separate initiatives quietly become one shared dependency.
